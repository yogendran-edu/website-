Refund Policy

At yrmservices.in, we strive to provide the best possible products and services to our customers. However, we understand that sometimes a product or service may not meet your expectations, and in those cases, we offer a refund policy as outlined below.

Eligibility for a Refund

In order to be eligible for a refund, the following criteria must be met:

  • The product or service must be returned within 15 days of the original purchase date.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team via email at info@yrmservices.in or by phone/whatsapp at +917028508805.
  2. Provide the order number and a detailed explanation of why you are requesting a refund.
  3. Our team will review your request and provide instructions on how to return the product or cancel the service.
  4. Once we receive the returned product or confirmation of service cancellation, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days.

Exceptions to the Refund Policy

In some cases, a refund may not be possible or may be subject to certain conditions. Examples include:

  • Services that have already been provided, such as consultations, cannot be refunded.
  • Refunds may be subject to a restocking fee or other charges, depending on the reason for the refund request.
